What We Do

Professional Services

We offer complete solutions to make your carpets and upholstery look and feel like new again.

🧹

Carpet Cleaning

We remove deep dirt, stains, and allergens using a high-powered truck-mounted steam unit. Visible results, fast drying.
🛋️

Upholstery Cleaning

Sofas, chairs, headboards and more. We remove stains, odors, and bacteria without damaging fabrics, leaving your furniture fresh and renewed.
↔️

Carpet Restretching

Ripples, bubbles, or wrinkles? We use power stretchers to eliminate looseness, reinstall tack strips and trim excess carpet for a perfect finish.
💧

Wather Damage / Restoration

Burst pipe or flood damage: we extract water, dry the space with dehumidifiers and air scrubbers, and assess repair options to save your home.
🏠
