What We Do

Professional Services

We offer complete solutions to make your carpets and upholstery look and feel like new again.

Carpet Cleaning

Carpet Cleaning

We remove deep dirt, stains, and allergens using a high-powered truck-mounted steam unit. Visible results, fast drying.

Upholstery Cleaning

Upholstery Cleaning

Sofas, chairs, headboards and more. We remove stains, odors, and bacteria without damaging fabrics, leaving your furniture fresh and renewed.
↔️ Popular Service

Carpet Re-stretching

Carpet Re-stretching

Ripples, bubbles, or wrinkles ruining the look of your floors? We use professional power stretchers to pull your carpet tight, reinstall tack strips, and trim any excess — leaving a flawless, like-new finish. Fast results, guaranteed.

Carpet Repair

Carpet Repair

Tears, burns, seams coming apart, or damaged sections? We patch and repair your carpet to restore its look and extend its life.

Stain Removal

Stain Removal

Pet stains, rust, red stains, blood, ink and more. Specialized treatments to eliminate even the toughest stains from carpets and upholstery.

Water Damage / Restoration

Water Damage / Restoration

Burst pipes, floods, storm damage. We extract water fast and fully dry the space using air movers, scrubbers and dehumidifiers to prevent mold.
